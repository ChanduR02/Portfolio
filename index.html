About Me

Dr. Venkata Reddy is a distinguished senior physiotherapist with a wealth of experience, having worked in multi-specialty hospitals since 2018. He graduated from Sims Medical University, where he received his doctorate in Physiotherapy. His expertise includes a wide range of physiotherapy techniques and patient care methodologies, making him a valuable asset to any medical team. Dr. Reddy is known for his patient-centered approach, commitment to continuous learning, and staying abreast of the latest advancements in physiotherapy. He is dedicated to providing the highest standard of care to his patients, helping them achieve optimal health and wellness.
